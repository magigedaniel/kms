About Us

KENGA MASSIVE SACCO LTD (KMS) was registered in the year 2020 as a co-operative society in response to the economic needs of local professionals and the general local populace.

Professionals from different sectors felt the compelling need to save together and build a society that can incorporate the local business people and help them access credit which had become difficult getting from the mainstream financial institutions which are scarce within the kuria region.

The common business people were then unable to access credit facilities from the mainstream financial institutions due to lack of collateral and other conditionalities. The Society was therefore a result of the vision and initiative of a few professionals who decided to harness their resources by creating a pool out of which members could draw affordable credit facility.

We offer well structured and affordable products and services geared at meeting the needs of our clientele who range from Professionals, Business people, micro-entrepreneurs, civil servants, Boda Boda sector and employees of all sectors. Due to the evolving nature of the industry, the KMS research and marketing team continually apply technology and innovation to develop products that address the needs of our members without compromising its financial sustainability. We look forward to talking to SASRA to allow us to become a deposit taking Sacco.

Chairman
